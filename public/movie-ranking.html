The Best Video Games

Tim's favorite video games

1. Battlefield 4

Battlefield 4 is by far the best game of all time. It has tons of guns, huge maps, levelution, and so much fun!

2. Minecraft

Minecraft is a fun block game that is made for all ages. It has adventure elements as well as creative projects and even combat.

3. Pokemon Platinum

Pokemon is a fan favorite game that many adults have grown up with. The game contains tons of monsters that you can collect which range from cute to cool, weak to strong, and common to ultra rare. Gotta catch em all!
